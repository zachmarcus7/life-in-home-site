Life in Home Team · Portland, OR

At this point in my business, I find that many of my clients are homeowners making a lifestyle move — young families growing into their next home, single women enjoying ownership adventures, couples transitioning to new phases in their lives. I enjoy the whole process of helping them sell their current home and then finding the next perfect one.

My past clients have voted for me as one of Portland Magazine's 5-Star Realtors every year from 2012 to 2022. I embrace my role as an advocate for my clients and work diligently to maintain an excellent reputation among my peers and industry affiliates.

Heather Bojanowski

Escrow Officer · Chicago Title Clackamas

Heather has been with the Chicago Title team for over 18 years and is practiced at all types of transactions. Colleagues and clients would describe her as friendly, professional, highly organized, and extremely hard-working. Her goal is to provide outstanding service to her clients, backed with years of experience and strong communication.

Originally from the Bay Area, Heather moved to Oregon in 2016 because she loves the rain and greenery — it's the most beautiful place she's ever lived. In her free time, she loves baking, reading, and most importantly spending time with her family and friends. If you're looking for an Escrow partner who truly cares, Heather is excited to work with you.
