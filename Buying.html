Our Process

Two clear phases from
search to signed

A focused, personal approach that puts your goals at the center of every decision.

01
Phase One

Figuring Out Your Ideal Home

  • In-depth conversation to understand your goals and motivations
  • Clarify must-haves vs. nice-to-haves for your search
  • Review of current market conditions in your target neighborhoods
  • Regular viewings scheduled — attended personally with expert advice
  • Investment analysis presented for every property of interest
02
Phase Two

Negotiating Your Best Deal

  • Active negotiation to secure the best possible price and terms
  • Contracts and transaction documents reviewed for precision
  • Referrals to Portland's best lenders, appraisers, and inspectors
  • Full support through due diligence and contingency periods
  • Present at every step from accepted offer to closing day
