About This Property

Beautiful, spacious, and move-in ready, this exceptional Happy Valley view home offers flexible living across three levels — ideal for entertaining, multi-generational living, or everyday comfort in style.

Featuring a spacious living room with soaring vaulted ceilings and custom upgrades throughout, this home showcases 7 bedrooms, 4 full baths, and over 4,100 sq ft. Enjoy a stunning updated kitchen, expansive view decks, a luxurious private primary retreat with flex space, and a fully equipped lower-level living suite.

Recent updates include quartz countertops, newer flooring, and a newer front-yard sprinkler system. Located in a highly desirable neighborhood with sweeping outlooks — a true showcase property.
