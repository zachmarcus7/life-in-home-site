About This Property

Beautifully maintained Southeast Portland home offering warm hardwood floors throughout the main level, abundant natural light, and inviting living spaces designed for comfort and connection.

The spacious living room features a stunning picture window and skylight, while the formal dining area opens to an expansive backyard deck. The kitchen showcases solid surface Corian countertops, abundant cabinetry, an electric glass-top range with convection oven, pantry storage, and water filtration at the sink.

The fully fenced backyard is a true garden retreat — a deck, gazebo, lawn, mature landscaping, and fruit trees await. Conveniently located near parks, shopping, and everyday amenities, you will love this home and can truly make it your own.
