About This Property

Gorgeous updated custom home settled in a cul-de-sac. You'll love the craftsman features, spacious rooms, gleaming laminate, marble, and tile floors — plus custom windows, skylights, and a vaulted ceiling throughout.

Four bedrooms include a master suite that opens directly to the deck and fenced yard. The custom-designed kitchen features soapstone countertops, custom cabinets, and a butcher block island — a cook's dream with both beauty and function.

The spacious great and family room features an Avalon Olympic wood stove and a dedicated office space. Outside, a landscaped lot with deck, hot tub, and garden shed completes the picture. Close to shops, buses, schools, and parks — a great home in a great neighborhood.
