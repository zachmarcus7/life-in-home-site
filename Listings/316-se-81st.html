About This Property

Step into this enchanting Montavilla residence with a Metal Standing Seam Interlock Roof boasting a lifetime warranty since 2019 — a perfect blend of durability and serenity. The exterior features Custom Hardy Plank replaced in 2019 and a new 20×9 porch with custom woodwork, offering enduring beauty and a tranquil spot to enjoy the surroundings.

Indoors, Custom Hemlock Molding installed in 2019 and updated in 2023 elevates the charm, while new Pella Windows flood the living spaces with natural light. Modern comfort is ensured by a Split Heating and Cooling Unit and Custom Craftsman Woodwork throughout. The primary bedroom sanctuary features New Designer Luxury Vinyl Plank Flooring, and the well-equipped kitchen includes all appliances. Safety is paramount with a Seismic Retrofitted Foundation and In-Wall Wired Ring Security System.

Outdoors, established flower gardens, a porch, patio, deck, and firepit lounge area create an idyllic space for relaxation. This Montavilla masterpiece seamlessly combines luxurious living, impeccable construction, and an enchanting garden — a haven poised to become your forever home.
