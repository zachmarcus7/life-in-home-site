About This Property

This warm and inviting home is perfect for family gatherings, everyday comfort, and easy living. Thoughtfully updated throughout, the kitchen shines with quartz countertops, modern finishes, and a layout designed for both functionality and style.

Enjoy multiple living spaces with two cozy areas, each featuring a gas fireplace—ideal for relaxing evenings or entertaining guests. Step outside to a spacious deck where you can take in breathtaking Mt. Hood views, or unwind in the beautifully landscaped backyard complete with a patio and garden beds.

Additional upgrades provide peace of mind and efficiency, including fully paid solar panels, a newer electrical panel, and updated siding with fresh exterior paint. With RV parking and a large two-car garage that includes workshop space, this home offers the flexibility and storage today's buyers are looking for.
