About This Property

Step into a truly special home bursting with charm, character, and an abundance of natural light. Nestled on a beautifully landscaped, fenced corner lot in Sellwood, this 4-bedroom, 1.5-bath vintage gem is filled with sunshine, soul, and smart updates throughout.

The cheerful living room features sunlight streaming through large picture-framed windows, bouncing off warm beech hardwood floors and highlighting the vintage knotty pine paneling. High ceilings and fresh paint (2021) make this space feel open, airy, and welcoming.

The dining room glows with light from three windows and a glass door opening to the side yard. With updated lighting, a charming picture-framed ceiling, and beautiful hardwoods, it's the perfect place for cozy meals or joyful gatherings.
