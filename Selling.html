Our Process

Three clear phases
from listing to closing

A transparent, step-by-step approach so you always know exactly where things stand.

01
Phase One

First Steps & Preparation

  • Complete walk-through of your home with our team
  • Staging recommendations tailored to your property
  • Professional photography scheduled and coordinated
  • Seller's Notes and Home Features document created
  • Final walk-through review before going live
02
Phase Two

Listing & Marketing

  • Listing signpost and Perma-Flyer installed
  • Property uploaded to MLS and RMLS
  • Email campaigns and postcard outreach
  • Social media announcements and targeted promotion
  • Open House events hosted and promoted
03
Phase Three

Offers & Closing

  • Agent and buyer feedback collected and reviewed
  • Regular progress reports — daily or weekly
  • Comparable pricing reviewed for ongoing competitiveness
  • Skilled negotiation to secure best possible terms
  • Smooth close with full transaction support
